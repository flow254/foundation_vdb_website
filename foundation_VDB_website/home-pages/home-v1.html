E-COMMERCE AND RETAIL
Increase revenue and improve customer loyalty by understanding your customers' preferences and how to best monetize them. Action on customer feedback and Improve your brand's perception across physical locations and online.

IOT AND CONNECTED DEVICES
VibraniumDB's open architecture allows you to seamlessly centralize and analyze all your data from sensors, industrial control systems and other devices. Take advantage of our seamless integrations and analysis to draw insights on the health of your system and on where you can reduce cost, increase system uptime and improve your organization's efficiency. Usher in Industry 4.0 with advanced analytics, intelligent reporting and automations that ensure your organization runs like a well-oiled machine.

SUPPLY CHAIN
VibraniumDB enables you to analyze and visualize your supply chain data to an item-level of granularity. Combined with advanced analytics, this detailed view of supply chain goods, costs and transit times allows enterprise decision-makers to optimize their cost of goods sold, warehousing, and profits item by item. These insights ensure better decision-making for invoices along with more predictable delivery times for your orders. Insights through Vibranium Data improve the customer experience and lead to higher rates of customer retention and a more positive brand presence for your business across both suppliers and customers.

CITIES AND PUBLIC SECTOR
Cities are becoming more connected than ever. The smart city is on the rise and data is its engine. Through Vibranium Data's open architecture, city officials and other decision-makers can combine insights from public and private datasets to drive policy and other forms of decision-making. VibraniumDB's platform supports IoT data from embedded systems along with data from cameras, social media, and even privately hosted city data that might come from third parties such as utilities or environmental organizations.
With VibraniumDB's seamless centralization and robust data ingestion, you and your team can access and share insights from anywhere across the world through the cloud. Contact us to learn how we can help your city to take more steps to becoming more connected and sustainable.
