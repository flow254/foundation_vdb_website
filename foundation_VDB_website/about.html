About us

About our firm

Vibranium Data is a Data Analytics company that helps businesses ranging from Fortune 500 companies to small and medium-sized businesses utilize their data resources for better decision-making.

Through our Open Architecture on VibraniumDB, insightful analysis and easy-to-read visualizations, we power your teams to make better decisions for your business.

Improved decision-making leads to increased revenue, cost savings and better long-term profitability. Our data models also help businesses improve their brand perception and reach, thereby helping your company step ahead of the competition.

Hosted on VibraniumDB TM , your data, custom models and dashboards are accessible across both desktop, mobile and web.
We are privacy-compliant and pride ourselves in our speed of execution, accurate delivery and data security.

Our process

02

pipelines

After learning about your business, we can use both your internal datasets and publicly available data to help you prioritize the insights you are looking for. We will then clean, integrate, analyze and visualize these datasets in a way that helps you see your business or market with greater clarity. This process end-to-end usually takes a minimum of six weeks from the beginning of the project. We pride ourselves in our speed, accuracy of delivery and realistic timelines.

01

PLANNING

We start with a preliminary call to understand the background of your business, what your data end goal is and what your current business KPIs are. We work with both technical and non-technical teams, so it's okay if you aren't sure of the end goal - we can partner with you to help refine this.

04

presentation

Throughout the process, we keep you in the loop by showing you unique insights about your business in easy to grasp visualizations. We hand off important tools and train both technical and non-technical team members on how to interact with our platform. We also include documentation and a data map of the various data sources we are integrating in helping you make your decisions.

Afterwards, we can be brought on as consultants for your data process in the future so as to help with new data problems or to uncover and interpret new insights.

We also work with some of our partners to publish case studies on their businesses after delivery, helping your company have more objective publications that can be presented to investors.

03

process

We understand that this is a partnership, and that lead metrics may change as you begin exploring the data or analyzing insights. We work with your team to iterate over dashboard concepts until you have a set of dashboards that your firm is absolutely comfortable with. We can create varying dashboards for different teams or business functions, from field workers to C-Suite executives.
